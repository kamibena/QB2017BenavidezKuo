Readme - bci_manual_rhcl

- The file bci_manual_rhcl.txt contains relative humidity data based on wet and dry bulb temperatures collected manually at
  the BCi clearing station.

- The wet and dry bulb temperatures are recorded with a sling psychrometer around mid-day, i.e. between 12:00 and 13:00.

- No values are collected on the weekends and holidays.
